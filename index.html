Precision Lawn Care & Maintenance

A lush, green lawn is the foundation of any beautiful landscape. Our maintenance programs are not one-size-fits-all; they are customized based on your specific grass type, soil composition, and local climate conditions. We understand that different regions of the USA require different care schedules, from the warm-season grasses of the South to the cool-season varieties of the North.

Our Maintenance Protocol Includes:

  • Strategic Mowing: We adhere to the "one-third rule," never cutting more than one-third of the grass blade at a time to prevent stress and promote deep root growth. Our equipment is regularly sharpened to ensure clean cuts that resist disease.
  • Edging and Trimming: Precision edging along driveways, sidewalks, and flower beds creates crisp, clean lines that define your landscape and give it a manicured, professional appearance.
  • Core Aeration: Over time, soil becomes compacted, restricting water and nutrient access to roots. Our core aeration service removes small plugs of soil to alleviate compaction, allowing your lawn to breathe and absorb fertilizers more effectively.
  • Overseeding: To combat thinning lawns and weed invasion, we introduce high-quality grass seed varieties that are resistant to drought and disease, ensuring a dense, vibrant turf.

We also offer seasonal cleanup services, including spring leaf removal and fall winterization, ensuring your lawn remains healthy through every cycle of the year.

Arboriculture: Tree Trimming & Removal

Trees are the long-term investment of your landscape. Proper care extends their lifespan, improves their structure, and ensures the safety of your property. Our certified arborists provide expert tree services that prioritize both the health of the tree and the safety of your home.

Tree Health & Safety Services:

  • Structural Pruning: Young trees benefit from structural pruning to establish a strong central leader and well-spaced scaffold branches. This prevents future structural failures and reduces the need for heavy corrective pruning later in life.
  • Crown Thinning & Raising: For mature trees, we perform crown thinning to improve light penetration and air circulation, which reduces the risk of fungal diseases. Crown raising clears lower branches to provide clearance for pedestrians, vehicles, and structures.
  • Hazardous Tree Removal: When a tree is dead, diseased, or structurally compromised, it poses a significant risk. Our team uses advanced rigging techniques and state-of-the-art equipment to safely remove hazardous trees, even in confined spaces, without damaging surrounding property.
  • Stump Grinding: After removal, we grind stumps below grade level, allowing you to replant or sod over the area, restoring the usability of your land.

Smart Irrigation & Water Management

Water is a precious resource, and efficient usage is critical for both environmental sustainability and cost savings. Our irrigation specialists design and install smart systems that deliver the right amount of water to the right place at the right time.

Irrigation Solutions:

  • Smart Controller Installation: We install Wi-Fi-enabled controllers that adjust watering schedules based on real-time weather data, preventing overwatering during rain and underwatering during heatwaves.
  • Drip Irrigation Systems: Ideal for flower beds, shrubs, and vegetable gardens, drip systems deliver water directly to the root zone, minimizing evaporation and runoff.
  • High-Efficiency Sprinkler Heads: We upgrade older systems with rotary nozzles and pressure-regulated heads that distribute water more evenly and efficiently.
  • Rain Sensor Integration: Automatic rain sensors shut off the system during precipitation, ensuring you never pay to water your lawn while it's raining.

Yes, absolutely. GreenScape Pro is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both our employees and your property. We are happy to provide certificates of insurance upon request.

What areas do you serve?
